Upcoming Event

Date 2017-03-16 四 Tags POST
We will be running a Medley Festival event during the following period. Play up to 3 songs in a row to earn points and rewards!

A new function to allow song re-selection has been added, as well as a new Special Effect!

Event Period:
From 9:00 am on Fri, March 17 to 8:00 am Mon, March 27 UTC.

- Medley Festivals completed after 8:00 am Monday, March 27 UTC will not be eligible for Event Pts. and other rewards may also be forfeit if the game enters maintenance.

Event Details
■ Playing the Medley Festival
・Make your selection
First select the number of songs you want to play in a medley between 1 to 3 songs. Then select the difficulty level you want to play and the songs to play will be automatically selected.

NEW FEATURE!
You can now re-select the song selection by using Gs.
- The attribute of the song selection will not change.
- It is possible to get the previously selected songs again.
- You can re-select up to ten times per Festival session.

・Event Songs
The songs you play in a medley are selected randomly every time you play. If you play more than one song, all consecutive songs will be of the same attribute as the first song, and the same songs will not be repeated for the duration of that medley session. There are eight different songs per attribute on EASY to EXPERT difficulty levels.

・Add Special Effects
Use Gs to add Special Effects to your festival.
These give you additional bonus effects during the performances.

NEW FEATURE!
A new Special Effect “Support Bonus” has been added. By using this Special Effect, you will are guaranteed to receive a boost from your friend or another student before each Live Show during a Festival session.

・Receive Support
Your Friends or other students may come to support you before you start the medley. They can give you a boost during performances.

・Get the Rewards
You will receive Event Pts. for successfully completing a medley.
The amount of Event Pts. you receive will vary depending on various factors, such as your final score, combos you managed to string, and Special Effects and support bonuses.

You can also receive Gold, Silver and Bronze rewards from the Medley Festival.
You are more likely to receive Gold and Silver rewards for completing the Medley Festival on higher difficulties.

Note:
- Your Stamina and combo string will get carried over to the next song if you play more than one song,
- If you fail to complete a medley, you will not receive the completion rewards and receive little or no Event Pts.

■ Event Rewards
You may receive event rewards for the total amount of Event Pts. you’ve collected, as well as ranking rewards for your ranking at the end of the event.

・Achievement Rewards
SR Honoka Kosaka [Sweet Cake]
R Satoko Miyama [Teacher]
and more...

・Score Ranking Rewards
SS Stickers
S Stickers
N Stickers

Rewards depend on:
- The total number of Event Points you accumulate.
- Your ranking based on your total Event Points.
- Your Score Ranking on every song you've played during the event. Your total Score per Medley Festival session is reflected to the ranking if you play two or more songs.

Note:
- SR Honoka Kosaka [Sweet Cake] is being released early for this event, and will be added to Honor Student scouting, SR/UR scouting, and SR or above μ's Scouting during April.
- SR Members released early for events have a lower appearance rate from Scouting than standard SR Members.
- Teachers are ideal for Practicing, but can't be added to a Team or be Idolized.
- The rewards will be distributed after the event has finished.

We hope you continue to enjoy Love Live! School Idol Festival.
