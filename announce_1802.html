Master Songs RevivalFan favorite Master songs are back!2017/08/01

Date 2017-08-01 二 Tags POST
As previously announced, we have started to re-release some of the Master songs. Starting from Tuesday, Aug 1 UTC, there will also be special time-limited Goals related to the Master songs. Don't miss out on their rewards!

■ Master Song Re-release Schedule
Sun, July 16 - Snow Halation
Fri, July 21 - Baby Maybe Koi no Button
Tue, August 1 - Natsu Owaranaide.
Sun, August 6 - Listen to My Heart!
Wed, August 16 - Binetsu kara Mystery
Mon, August 21 - Cutie Panther
Fri, September 1 - After School NAVIGATORS
Wed, September 6 - Wild Stars

Notes
- Each Master song is released at 12:00 am UTC on their re-release dates.
- Master songs are added to μ’s B-Sides.
- Available periods for "Natsu Owaranaide." (since Wed, June 21) and "Binetsu kara Mystery" (since Sat, July 1) will be extended during scheduled maintenances.
- Master song selection and re-release timing may change without prior notice.

■ Time-Limited Goals
Achieve these special Goals to gain Love Gems and Gs! They become available in batches as listed below, but will all end at 11:59 pm on Saturday, September 30 UTC.

Batch 1 - 12:00 am, Tue, August 1 UTC
Batch 2 - 12:00 am, Sun, August 6 UTC
Batch 3 - 12:00 am, Wed, August 16 UTC
Batch 4 - 12:00 am, Mon, August 21 UTC
Batch 5 - 12:00 am, Fri, September 1 UTC
Batch 6 - 12:00 am, Wed, September 6 UTC

Notes
- Please view the "Goals" section of the game for more information on progress and rewards.
- Unachieved time-limited Goals will be considered Not Cleared when their availability ends.
- Full Combo count you already have prior to this event will not count towards these time-limited Goals.

We hope you continue to enjoy Love Live! School Idol Festival.
