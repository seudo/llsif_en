New Event Notice

Date 2015-06-18 四 Tags REMOVE
EVENT DETAILS

During the event, special "Parasol" rhythm icons will appear in all Live Shows. Tap these icons to get Parasols and Event Points. Use Parasols to play the B-Side “Arifureta Kanashimi no Hate”, where you can get more than the normal amount of Event Points.
The amount of Event Points you can get in “Arifureta Kanashimi no Hate” depends on your Score and Combo Rank.
We plan to add “Arifureta Kanashimi no Hate” as a Hit song sometime after the event has finished.

Note: You will not receive Event Points for Live Shows completed after 8:00 am on Tuesday, June 30 UTC, even if you start before this time.

Depending on the total Event Points collected, you will be able to enjoy the special event story for Eli.

REWARDS
Eli Ayase [Passing Rain] (SR)
Kyoko Sasahara [Teacher] (R)
Nanako Yamauchi [Teacher] (R)
Alpaca (R)
and many more!

Eli Ayase [Passing Rain] (SR) is being released early for this event, and will be added as an Honor Student in mid-July. SR Members released early for events have a lower appearance rate from Scouting than standard SR Members. The Alpacas can be Removed in exchange for G or used for Practicing, and the Teachers are ideal for Practicing, but neither can be added to a Team or be Idolized.

Rewards depend on:
- The total number of Event Points you accumulate.
- Your Event Ranking at 8:00 am on June 30 (UTC).
- Your "Arifureta Kanashimi no Hate" Song Ranking at 8:00 am on June 30 (UTC).

Note: Rewards will be distributed after the event has finished.

We are also running the following specials from 12:00 am on June 27 UTC until the end of the event.

x4 Event Songs
Four times the amount of Parasols is required to play these songs, but you will also receive four times the Event Pts.
Live rewards such as EXP, G, number of Members, and Member levels are the same as for regular event songs.

Expert Event Song
A higher difficulty version of the event song is available under B-Sides, Expert.
75 Parasols required.

Random Notes Expert Event Song
The same Expert Event Song as above, but the Notes are randomized!

Note: Playing any of the three song versions above will not count towards the Event Song Ranking.

Extra Bonus G
You can earn extra G from certain songs: 150% from Normal, and 200% from Hard.
Eligible songs change every day.
