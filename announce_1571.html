Popular Selection Scouting

Date 2017-06-15 四 Tags POST
Popular Selection scouting will be available from 12:00 am on Tuesday, June 13 to 7:59 am on Friday, June 16 UTC. The selection will change daily. Scout 11 on Popular Selection Scouting guarantees an SR or above member! Also, there will be a daily selection of members from a specific series with increased appearance rates!

■ Tuesday, June 13 - Kimono series
- Finished -

■ Wednesday, June 14 - Kung Fu Star series
- Finished -

■ Thursday, June 15 - Valentines series
・A selection of SR/UR members released between November 2015 to February 2016.
Winter Sports, Goddesses of Fortune, Valentines, White Day.
・A selection of Event SR members released between November 2015 to March 2016.
Santa, Angel, New Year Feast (Kotori), Snow Fairy (Hanayo), Setsubun (Honoka), Snow Rabbit, Hinamatsuri (Eli), Playing Catch.
・All R members ever released.

■ Friday, June 16 (until 7:59 am) - Kunoichi series
・A selection of SR/UR members released between June to August 2016.
Swimming Pool, Kunoichi, Animal Part 2, Dance Party.
・A selection of Event SR members released between June to October 2016.
Bath Robe, Track and Field, Festival [Kotori], Harvest Moon [Hanayo], Rock! [Maki], Steam Punk, Autumn Leaves, Explorer.
・All R members ever released.

Notes
- Individual member appearance rates are equal among each rarity except for the following:

[Appearance rate increased members]
Regardless of the total number of members available, these members have a flat rate increase in appearance rate per member within each rarity.

[Event released members]
Each member's appearance rate is reduced to one-fifth.
- See the "Details" section of each scouting for more info on which members appear.

We hope you continue to enjoy Love Live! School Idol Festival.
