New Members Added

Date 2016-10-10 一 Tags POST
We have added new members with the maintenance that started at 8:00 am on Monday, October 10 UTC.

The μ's members are looking good in their excercise wear in this series. Idolize them into baseball themed stage costumes. Don't forget to check out the brand new side stories too!

These newly added members have increased appearance rates from the Honor Student Scouting until 8:00 am on Friday, October 14 UTC.

Newly added members to μ's Honor Student Scouting and SR/UR Scouting.
UR Maki Nishikino [Baseball]
SR Honoka Kosaka [Baseball]
SR Rin Hoshizora [Baseball]
SR Nozomi Tojo [Baseball]
SR Hanayo Koizumi [Baseball]
SR Hanayo Koizumi [Harvest Moon]*

We are also running a special promotion at the same time, where "Scout 11" Honor Student Scouting guarantees an SR or UR member.

Regular Student Scouting also contain some new members too, so don’t forget to give Regular Student Scouting a try as well.

N Ryo Aikawa
N Tsurugi Kadota*

Note
- The members marked with * are past event members with lower appearance rates.
- Appearance rates for the newly added members are higher than the others. However, the appearance rates for UR and SR rarities remain unchanged.
- The increased appearance rate promotion does not apply to SR Hanayo Koizumi [Harvest Moon].
- The SR and UR members newly added to the Honor Student Scouting are also available from the SR/UR Scouting, but the appearance rates are not increased.
- We have made changes to the Honor Student Scouting lineup. Please see the notification titled "Update Notice: October 10" for more details.
- Rarity appearance rates within each Scouting are fixed and does not change.
- Individual member appearance rates are equal among each rarity except for the following:

[Appearance rate increased members in Honor Student Scouting]
Regardless of the total number of members available, these members have a flat rate increase in appearance rate per member within each rarity.

[Event released members]
Each member's appearance rate is reduced to one-fifth.

- The new members added to the Honor Student Scouting are also available from the following, but appearance rates are not increased.
・SR/UR Scouting (SSR members are not available)
・SR or above μ's Scouting

Please check the Details found on the scouting page for more information.

We hope you continue to enjoy Love Live! School Idol Festival.
