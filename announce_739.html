New Club Members Added

Date 2016-07-25 一 Tags POST
We have added new members with the maintenance that started at 8:00 am on Monday, July 25 UTC.

μ's in animal hoodies are so irresistibly cute! Idolize them into even cuter animal themed stage costumes. Don't forget to check out the brand new side stories too!

Newly added members to Honor Student Scouting and SR/UR Scouting.
UR Maki Nishikino [Animal Part 2]
UR Honoka Kosaka [Animal Part 2]
SR Eli Ayase [Animal Part 2]
SR Kotori Minami [Animal Part 2]
SR Rin Hoshizora [Animal Part 2]
SR Umi Sonoda [Animal Part 2]
SR Nozomi Tojo [Animal Part 2]
SR Hanayo Koizumi [Animal Part 2]
SR Nico Yazawa [Animal Part 2]
SR Nozomi Tojo [Bath Robe]*

These members except for SR Nozomi Tojo [Bath Robe], have increased appearance rates from the Honor Student Scouting until 8:00 am on Friday, July 29 UTC.

We are also running a special promotion at the same time, where "Scout 11" Honor Student Scouting guarantees an SR or above member.

Regular Student Scouting also contains some new members too, so don't forget to give Regular Student Scouting a try as well.

N Coco Miyashita
N Yuri Mido
N Akiru Shinomiya*

Note:
- The members marked with * are past event members with lower appearance rates.
- Appearance rates for the newly added members are higher than the others. However, the appearance rates for UR and SR rarities remain unchanged.
- The increased appearance rates with the Honor Student Scouting and SR/UR Scouting does not apply to SR Nozomi Tojo [Bath Robe].
- The SR and UR members newly added to the Honor Student Scouting are also available from the SR/UR Scouting, but the appearance rates are not increased.
- We are removing older selection of members from the Honor Student Scouting in order to allow more chance for the newer members to appear. Please see the notification "Update Notice: July 25" for more details on what has changed this month.
- Appearance rates from Regular Student Scouting remain unchanged.

We hope you continue to enjoy Love Live! School Idol Festival.
