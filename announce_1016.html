Skill Related Adjustments

Date 2016-12-07 三 Tags POST
We will make the following adjustments to the way Skills level up for UR and SSR members.

Scheduled update maintenance:
Thursday, December 8 from 8:00 am until 11:00 am UTC

Update details:
1. Required Skill experience points reduced for leveling them up
There will be less Skill experience points required to level up the Skills of UR and SSR members. The UR and SSR members you already own will be automatically adjusted with the new reduced requirements.

If the experience points exceed Level 8 after the adjustment, you will be given Supporting Members of the same Attribute according to the amount of excess experience points.

EXAMPLE - Excess of 2,150 Skill experience points on a Smile Attribute member will equate to UR Nico's Mother x2, SR Mika x1, R Cocoa Yazawa x5.

Available Supporting Members by Attributes
Smile: UR Nico's Mother / SR Mika / R Cocoa Yazawa
Pure: UR Kotori's Mother / SR Fumiko / R Cotaro Yazawa
Cool: UR Maki's Mother / SR Hideko / R Cocoro Yazawa

Note:
- UR Special Reward / Login Bonus members and regular SR / R members are not eligible for this adjustment.

2. Skill trigger rates and effects up adjusted for leveling them up
Skills of UR and SSR members added between May 2014 to November 2016 will receive increased Skill trigger rates and Skill effects for each level they are levelled up. UR / SSR members added after 11:00 am on Thursday, December 8 UTC will have the new rates applied already.

Note:
- Skill Lv. 1 is not affected by this adjustment.
- Some eligible members will receive either one of "increased Skill trigger rate" or "increased effect" only.
- UR Special Reward / Login Bonus members and regular SR / R members are not eligible for this adjustment.

We apologize for any inconveniences this may cause, and thank you for your understanding.


December 7, 2016
