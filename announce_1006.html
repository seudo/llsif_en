New μ's Members Added

Date 2016-12-05 一 Tags POST
We have added new μ's members at 9:00 am on Monday, December 5 UTC.

μ's members are dressed as carol singers in this series. Idolize them into Christmas-themed stage costumes! In the brand new side stories, they will be singing hymns at the local kindergarten. Celebrate the joy of the season with μ's!

Newly added members to μ's Honor Student Scouting:
UR Eli Ayase [Christmas]
SR Umi Sonoda [Christmas]
SR Rin Hoshizora [Christmas]
SR Maki Nishikino [Christmas]
SR Nozomi Tojo [Christmas]

These newly added members have increased appearance rates from the μ's Honor Student Scouting until 8:00 am on Thursday, December 8 UTC. We are also running the μ's "Scout 11" Limited Time Scouting with a guaranteed SR or above member promotion!

Note:
- Please bear in mind when scouting, that the change will occur automatically at 9:00 am on Monday, December 5 UTC.
- Individual member appearance rates are equal among each rarity except for the following:

[Appearance rate increased members in Honor Student Scouting]
Regardless of the total number of members available, these members have a flat rate increase in appearance rate per member within each rarity.

- The newly added members to Honor Student Scouting are also available from SR/UR Scouting (no SSR), SR or above μ's Scouting, and SSR or above μ's Scouting.
- Increased appearance rates of the new members don't apply to SR/UR Scouting, SR or above μ's Scouting, and SSR or above μ's Scouting.

Please check the Details found on the scouting page for more information.
