New Event

Date 2015-01-19 一 Tags POST
EVENT DETAILS

During the event, special "Amulet" rhythm icons will appear in all Live Shows. Tap these icons to get Amulets and Event Points. Use Amulets to play the B-Side “Binetsu kara Mystery”, where you can get more than the normal amount of Event Points.

The amount of Event Points you can get in “Binetsu kara Mystery” depends on your Score and Combo Rank.

We plan to add “Binetsu kara Mystery” as a Hit song sometime after the event has finished.

You will not receive Event Points for Live Shows completed after 8:00 am on January 30 UTC, even if you start before this time.

REWARDS

Nozomi Tojo (SR)
Kyoko Sasahara [Teacher] (R)
Satoko Miyama [Teacher] (R)
Alpaca (R)
and many more!

Nozomi Tojo (SR) is being released early for this event, and will be added as an Honor Student in mid-February. SR Members released early for events have a lower appearance rate from Scouting than standard SR Members. The Alpacas can be Removed in exchange for G or used for Practicing, and the Teachers are ideal for Practicing, but neither can be added to a Team or be Idolized.

Rewards depend on:
- The total number of Event Points you accumulate.
- Your Event Ranking at 8:00 am on January 30 UTC.
- Your “Binetsu kara Mystery” Song Ranking at 8:00 am on January 30 UTC.

Rewards will be distributed after the event has finished.

In addition, we are also running the following specials from 12:00 am on January 27 UTC until the end of the event.

- x4 Event Songs
Four times the amount of Amulets are required to play these songs, but you will also receive four times the Event Pts.

Live rewards such as EXP, G, number of Members, and Member levels are the same as for regular event songs.

- Expert Event Song
A higher difficulty version of the event song is available under B-Sides, Expert.
75 Amulets required.

Playing either of the two versions above will not count towards the Event Song Ranking.

- Extra Bonus G
You can earn extra Gs from certain songs: 150% from Normal, and 200% from Hard.
Eligible songs change every day.
