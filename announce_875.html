Upcoming New Event

Date 2016-10-13 四 Tags POST
This event will run from 9:00 am on Friday, October 14 to 8:00 am on Monday, October 24 UTC.
During the event, special "" tokens inside rhythm icons will appear in all Live Shows. Tap these icons to collect them and also get Event Points. Use the tokens you collect to play the event song, which will give you more Event Points compared to other songs.

Note:
- The amount of Event Points you can get from the event song depends on your Score and Combo Rank.
- You will not receive Event Points for Live Shows completed after 8:00 am on Monday, October 24 UTC, even if you start before this time.
- We plan to add the event song as a Hit song sometime after the event has finished.
- There is no event story to go with this event.

Event Achievement Rewards
SR Nico Yazawa [Autumn Leaves]
R Satoko Miyama [Teacher]
and many more!

Note:
- SR Nico Yazawa [Autumn Leaves] is being released early for this event, and will be added to Honor Student scouting, SR/UR scouting, and SR or above μ's Scouting during November.
- SR Members released early for events have a lower appearance rate from Scouting than standard SR Members.
- Teachers are ideal for Practicing, but can't be added to a Team or be Idolized.

Rewards depend on:
- The total number of Event Points you accumulate.
- Your ranking based on your total Event Points.
- Your Event Song ranking.

Note:
- For more details on achievement rewards and ranking rewards, please see the in-game event rewards section.
- Ranking rewards will be distributed after the event has finished.

The event tokens required to play the event song:
EASY - 15
NORMAL - 30
HARD - 45
EXPERT/RANDOM EXPERT - 75

X4 Event Song
From 12:00 am on Thursday October 20 until the end of the event.
Four times the amount of tokens are required to play these songs, but you will also receive four times the Event Points.

The event tokens required to play the X4 song:
EASY - 60
NORMAL - 120
HARD - 180
EXPERT/RANDOM EXPERT - 300

Note:
- The notes are randomized for Random Expert event songs.
- Only Expert event songs are available on Random.
- Live rewards such as EXP, G, number of Members, and Member levels are the same as for regular event songs.

Extra Bonus Gs
From 12:00 am on Friday, October 21 until the end of the event.
Earn extra Gs from certain songs.

Hits NORMAL - 150%
Hits HARD - 200%
Hits EXPERT - 300%
B-Sides EXPERT - 300%
B-Sides MASTER - 300%

We hope you continue to enjoy Love Live! School Idol Festival.
