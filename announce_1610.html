Team Scouting

Date 2017-06-27 二 Tags POST
A Team Scouting that features "lily white" members only will be available from 12:00 am on Tuesday, June 27 to 11:59 pm on Thursday, June 29 UTC.

Scout 11 on "lily white" Team Scouting guarantees an SR or above member! Also, there will be a daily selection of members from a specific series with increased appearance rates!

■ Tuesday, June 27
Increased chance for the White Day series!

UR Umi Sonoda "One Feeling"
Leader Skill: Smile Empress
Receive Smile Points bonus equivalent to 12% of Cool Points
+ additional 6% Smile Points bonus to second year members.
　
SR Rin Hoshizora "Shiny Chocolate"
SR Nozomi Tojo "Teddy Bears"

■ Wednesday, June 28
Increased chance for the Sports series!

UR Rin Hoshizora "Go for the Goal!"
Leader Skill: Cool Empress
Receive 9% Cool Points bonus
+ additional 3% Cool Points bonus to μ's members.

SR Umi Sonoda "Apt Manager"
SR Nozomi Tojo "Victory Prayer"

■ Thursday, June 29
Increased chance for the Summer Uniform series!

UR Nozomi Tojo "Fruit of Summer"
Leader Skill: Smile Princess
Receive 9% Smile Points bonus
+ additional 3% Smile Points bonus to μ's members.

SR Umi Sonoda "Today is Umi's Day"
SR Rin Hoshizora "Summer Sunset Girl"

Notes
- Due to change in the total number of members available, the individual appearance rates per member have changed.
- Individual member appearance rates are equal among each rarity except for the following:

[Appearance rate increased members]
Regardless of the total number of members available, these members have a flat rate increase in appearance rate per member within each rarity.

[Event released members]
Each member's appearance rate is reduced to one-fifth.

- See the "Details" section of each scouting for more info on which members appear.
- These scouts do not contain the new members that are scheduled to be added on Wednesday, June 28, 2017 UTC.

We hope you continue to enjoy Love Live! School Idol Festival.
