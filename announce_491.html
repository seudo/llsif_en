Limited Time Scouting Now Open!

Date 2015-12-08 二 Tags REMOVE
Special Limited Time Scouting containing only certain µ's members will be available from 12:00 am on Wednesday, December 9 to 8:00 am on Thursday, December 17 UTC.

There will be three rounds to this special scouting:

Round 1
12:00 am on Wednesday, December 9 to 11:59 pm on Friday, December 11

Round 2
12:00 am on Saturday, December 12 to 11:59 pm on Monday, December 14

Round 3
12:00 am on Tuesday, December 15 to 8:00 am on Thursday, December 17

For more details on which members appear, please check the Details found in the scouting page.

We are also running a special promotion at the same time, where 11 Limited Time Scouting guarantees an SR or above member.
Please be aware that we have changed the Scouting menu design so that when you press the Scouting button on the main menu while a Limited Scouting is available, the Limited Scouting will show up first.

We hope you continue to enjoy Love Live! School Idol Festival.
