Summer Holiday Event

Date 2017-08-11 五 Tags POST
New Aqours song and time-limited Goals!

We have started the Summer Holiday Event from Friday, August 11 UTC. Play the new song by Aqours and achieve special Goals for rewards! The new song and the special Goals are available for a limited time only, so don't miss out♪

■ Time-limited Song
Landing action Yeah!! (E/N/H/EX)
Added to Aqours B-Sides.

Available period
From 12:00 am on Fri, August 11 until 7:59 am on Fri, August 25 UTC.

■ Time-limited Goals
Each Goal will give you a Love Gem when completed.

Part 1
Summer Holiday Mission 1: Clear a Live Show once.
Summer Holiday Mission 2: Clear a Live Show ten times.

Available period
From 12:00 am on Fri, August 11 until 7:59 am on Fri, August 18 UTC.

Part 2
Summer Holiday Mission 3: Get a Full Combo once.
Summer Holiday Mission 4: Get a Full Combo five times.

Available period
From 12:00 am on Sat, August 12 until 7:59 am on Fri, August 18 UTC.

Part 3
Summer Holiday Mission 5: Clear Landing action Yeah!! on EASY.
Summer Holiday Mission 6: Clear Landing action Yeah!! on HARD.

Available period
From 12:00 am on Sun, August 13 until 7:59 am on Fri, August 18 UTC.

Notes
- Please view the "Goals" section of the game for more information on progress and rewards.
- The Full Combos required for Summer Holiday Missions 3 and 4 must be achieved between 12:00 am on Sat, August 12 to 7:59 am on Fri, August 18 UTC. Full Combo count you already have prior to this period will not count towards these Goals.
- Unachieved time-limited Goals will be considered Not Cleared when their availability period ends.

We hope you continue to enjoy Love Live! School Idol Festival.
