New Aqours Members Added

Date 2017-04-11 二 Tags POST
We added new members with the maintenance that started at 8:00 am on Tuesday, April 11 UTC.

Haregi is a type of kimono worn for special occasions, and Aqours members are doing just that for New Year’s! Idolize them into gorgeous kimono themed stage costumes! Find out what they are up to during New Year’s by unlocking their Side Stories♪

■ Members newly added to Aqours Honor Student Scouting:
UR Ruby Kurosawa [Haregi]
SSR Riko Sakurauchi [Haregi]
SR Kanan Matsuura [Haregi]
SR Yoshiko Tsushima [Haregi]

These newly added members have increased appearance rates from the Honor Student Scouting until 7:59 am on Tuesday, April 18 UTC. Also don't forget, "Scout 11" guarantees an SR or above member!

■ Member newly added to μ's/Aqours Regular Student Scouting:
N Haruka Konoe

Notes
- There are no event released members with this update.
- Appearance rates by rarity are fixed per scout type and do not change.
- Individual member appearance rates are equal among each rarity except for the following:

[Appearance rate increased members in Honor Student Scouting]
Regardless of the total number of members available, these members have a flat rate increase in appearance rate per member within each rarity.

- Due to changes in the total number of members available, the appearance rate of each member has changed.
- The SR and above members newly added to the Honor Student Scouting are also available from "SR or above Aqours Scouting" and "SSR or above Aqours Scouting", but the appearance rates are not increased.
- Rarity appearance rates within each Scouting are fixed and do not change.
- Appearance rates for Regular Student Scouting remain unchanged.
- See the "Details" section of each scouting for more info on which members appear.

We hope you continue to enjoy Love Live! School Idol Festival.
