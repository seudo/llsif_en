Update Notice: July 31

Date 2015-07-31 五 Tags POST
The following updates were made during the maintenance that started at 8:00 am on Friday, July 31 UTC.

- New Stories added.
- New Club Members added.
- Scouting Coupon feature added.
- Made changes to Honor Student Scouting.

Please see below for more details.

- New Stories Added.

Chapter 22 Story 3 and Story 4 were added.
View the Main Story up to Chapter 22 Story 2, then fulfill the Goals to view the new Stories.
Viewing the new Stories will also unlock new songs.

- Scouting Coupon feature added.

You will receive a Scouting Coupon every 20 Ranks.
If you are already over Rank. 20, you will receive your entitled Scouting Coupons a few days after Friday, July 31.
The Scouting Bonus Reward has also been changed from Love Gems to Scouting Coupons. Perform Honor Student Scouting or Time-Limited Scouting 10 times to receive a Scouting Coupon. Scouting Coupons can be used for two types of newly added special scouting.

[Supporting Member Scouting]
Use Supporting Members as a practice partner for a member of the same Attribute and Rarity for a chance to increase their special skill level. If either doesn't match, you will only receive EXP.The Skill levels of the supporting members can be increased in the same manner. The higher the skill level, the better the chance of increasing the skill level when used as a practice partner.When the skill levels of the base member and the practice partner are the same, the skill level of the base member will always go up.
Some supporting members are compatible with all Attributes.
For more details on which members appear, please check the Details found on the scouting page.
Requires 1 Scouting Coupon.

[SR/UR Only Scouting]
As the name suggest, you can only scout SR or UR Club Members. SR/UR members who were previously available from events have lower appearance rates.
For more details on which members appear, please check the Details found on the scouting page.
Requires 5 Scouting Coupons.

- Changes to Honor Student Scouting.

In order to offer better chances to scout newer SR/UR Club Members, we are limiting the SR/UR members that can be scouted from Honor Student Scouting to those who were added since Friday, January 30, 2015.

Note
The SR/UR members that are being removed from the Honor Student Scouting are still available from other time-limited scouting.
The same adjustment will occur at the end of every month.
