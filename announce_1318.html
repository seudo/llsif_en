Upcoming Event

Date 2017-03-31 五 Tags POST
We will be running a Score Match event during the following period. Earn Event Points to get the special SR member! We have made some functional adjustments to Score Match, please see the "Changes" section for more details.

Event Period:
From 9:00 am on Sat, April 1 to 8:00 am Tue, April 11 UTC.

- You will not receive Event Points for Live Shows completed after 8:00 am on Tuesday, April 11 UTC, even if you start before this time.

Event Details:
■ Four Player Face-off Match
Choose a difficultly level, and you will be automatically matched with up to three other players. After a random Live Show has been selected, all players will start the Live Show at the same time. At the end of the Live Show, you will be ranked according to your score, and you will receive Event Points based on the difficulty level and your rank.

■ Score Match Pts.
You will receive more Score Match Pts. if you beat players who are higher than you on the Score Match Pts. Ranking. You will receive only a small amount of Score Match Pts, for beating players who are lower than you, or even lose points depending on which place you come in on a match. Higher difficulty songs will give you or take away more points.

You can communicate with the other players by using set phrases while you are being matched, and when the results are displayed.

■ Changes
・The amount of Score Match Pts. you gain or lose has been adjusted.
・The timing of LP deduction has been changed from when the Live Show starts to when you select a difficulty. LP will remain spent if you force quit the application after selecting a difficulty and before completing the Live Show.

Note:
- If there are not enough players to start a match, computer players will be added.
- If you play the same difficulty in a row, you are less likely to get the same songs repeatedly.
- If the game enters maintenance during a Live Show, you will not receive Exp, G, and any other rewards you might have received.
- You cannot choose to continue a Score Match Live Show.

■ Achievement Rewards
SR You Watanabe [Helping Out]
R Kyoko Sasahara [Teacher]
R Nanako Yamauchi [Teacher]
and more!

■ Ranking Rewards
SR Chika Takami [Helping Out]
and more!

■ Score Ranking Rewards
SS Stickers
S Stickers
N Stickers

Rewards depend on:
- The total number of Event Points you accumulate.
- Your ranking based on your total Event Points.
- Your Event Song Ranking.
- Your Score Ranking on every song you've played during the event.

You can check out the top ten players and the team they've used on the Score Ranking page. Studying them might give you some clues on achieving a higher score!

Note:
- SR You Watanabe [Helping Out] and SR Chika Takami [Helping Out] are being released early for this event, and will be added to Honor Student scouting, SR/UR scouting, and SR or above μ's Scouting during April.
- SR Club Members released early for events have a lower appearance rate from Scouting than standard SR Members.
- Teachers are ideal for Practicing, but can't be added to a Team or be Idolized.
- Rewards will be distributed after the event has finished.

■ What is the "TECHNICAL" difficulty level?
Only the hardest of the Expert songs are selected.
Required LP and the rewards you could gain are the same as the EXPERT level.

■ Song Selections
Live songs "Jingle Bells ga Tomaranai" is specially made available in advance throughout the event duration.

The following numbers of songs are available for the difficulties as indicated.
・EASY/NORMAL/HARD/EXPERT/TECHNICAL - 17 songs each

The songs featured on TECHNICAL are selected from Random EXPERT songs (Stage Level ★9 or above.)

・Selection 1
From 9:00 am on Sat, April 1 to 7:59 am on Tue, April 4.
8 songs each on EASY, NORMAL, HARD, 8 songs on EXPERT, 8 songs on TECHNICAL.

・Selection 2
From 8:00 am on Tue, April 4 to 7:59 am on Fri, April 7.
Fully replace Selection 1 with 8 different songs each on EASY, NORMAL, HARD, 8 different songs on EXPERT, and 8 different songs on TECHNICAL.

・Selection 3
From 8:00 am on Fri, April 7 to 8:00 am on Tue, April 11.
Selection 1 + 2 all together!

Note:
- The pattern of Rhythm Icons are totally randomized for Random EXPERT songs. However, matched players will all see the same set of randomized Rhythm Icons.
- The songs available for Random EXPERT are the same as EXPERT songs.
- Random EXPERT may include songs that are being released as a random song for the first time.
- The song "Jingle Bells ga Tomaranai" will be available throughout the event period.

We hope you continue to enjoy Love Live! School Idol Festival.
