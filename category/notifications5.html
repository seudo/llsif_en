Limited Scouting Issue

There was an issue with the recent Pure Attribute Scouting, where Nozomi Tojo [Dancing Psychic] (SR) was not available from the Pure attributes only limited scouting.Due to the issue, we have closed the Pure Attribute Scouting at 4:30 am on Thursday, December 29 UTC.Instead of Smile Attribute Scouting planned run on Friday, December 30 UTC, Cool Attribute Scouting will be available from 12:00 am on Friday, December 30 to 11:59 pm on Saturday, December 31 UTC.We will refund all Love Gems that were spent on the Pure Attribute Scouting from 12:00 am until 4:30 am on Thursday, December 29 UTC.We aim to complete the delivery by the end of Friday, December 30 UTC.We apologize for any inconvenience this may have caused, and thank you for your understanding.December 29, 2016 2016/12/29

IMPORTANT In-App Purchase Price Change
