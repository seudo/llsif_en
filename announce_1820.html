Aqours 2nd Love Live! Promotion

Date 2017-08-03 四 Tags POST
To celebrate the commencement of Aqours 2nd Love Live! HAPPY PARTY TRAIN TOUR, we are currently running the countdown login bonus. In addition, we will be introducing the following!

1. Aqours 2nd Love Live! Special Login Bonus
Log in to the game during the two days the live show is performed to receive a Scouting Ticket each!

Available Periods
Nagoya: Sat, August 5 and Sun, August 6.
Kobe: Sat, August 19 and Sun, August 20.
Saitama: Fri, September 29 and Sat, September 30.

Special Login Bonus Details
First log in - 1 Scouting Ticket
Second log in - 1 Scouting Ticket

2. Aqours 2nd Live Pack
Special packs filled with great items will be on sale for a limited period to coincide with the live shows. These items can be purchased by tapping the banner in the Shop.

Available Periods
・Aqours 2nd Live Pack: Nagoya
From 9:00 am on Fri, August 4 until 11:59 pm on Mon, August 7 UTC.

・Aqours 2nd Live Pack: Kobe
From 9:00 am on Fri, August 18 until 11:59 pm on Mon, August 21 UTC.

・Aqours 2nd Live Pack: Saitama
From 9:00 am on Thu, September 28 until 11:59 pm on Sun, October 1 UTC.

Each pack contains the following items:
・Scout 11 Scouting Ticket x1
・SR or above Aqours Scouting Ticket x1
・SR Shiitake x5
・2nd Live Title: Nagoya

Item details
・Scout 11 Scouting Ticket
A ticket for a Scout 11 scouting.
You will receive Honor Student Bonus by using this ticket.

・SR or above Aqours Scouting Ticket
A ticket for a single scouting on Aqours SR or above scouting.
Features SR or above Aqours members that have been available from Honor Student Scouting since September 2016. You will NOT receive Honor Student Bonus by using this ticket.

・SR Shiitake
Shiitake is a Support Member who can't be added to a Team or be Idolized, but can boost a Club Member greatly when used as a Practice partner.

・Titles
“Happy Party Train Tour: Nagoya”
“Happy Party Train Tour: Kobe”
“Happy Party Train Tour: Saitama”
Very special Titles to commemorate the Aqours 2nd Love Live! HAPPY PARTY TRAIN TOUR. The Titles are unique to each show location!

Notes
- This pack can only be purchased once per person during the available period.
- This pack can only be purchased via payment methods accepted by respective application stores. Love Gems cannot be used.
- Scout 11 Scouting Ticket and SR or above Aqours Scouting Ticket will be sent directly to your inventory and not to your Present Box. The Support Member and the Title are sent to your Present Box.
- The ticket specific scouting will become available only when you hold an eligible ticket.

3. Title “Happy Party Train Tour” in Sticker Shop
A special Title to commemorate the Aqours 2nd Love Live! HAPPY PARTY TRAIN TOUR will be available in the Sticker Shop. Set this Title to your profile to show your support!

Available Periods
・During the Nagoya show
From 12:00 am on Sat, August 5 until 11:59 pm on Sun, August 6 UTC.

・During the Kobe show
From 12:00 am on Sat, August 19 until 11:59 pm on Sun, August 20 UTC.

・During the Saitama show
From 12:00 am on Fri, September 29 onward.

Required Stickers: N Stickers x5

Notes
- This Title can only be exchanged once per person during the available period.
- There is only one kind of this special Title, which is different from the Titles that are included in the Aqours 2nd Live Packs.

We hope you enjoy the special packs, and thank you for playing School Idol Festival!
