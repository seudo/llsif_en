To celebrate the major update, various packs have been added to the Shop! They can be purchased from the banner in the Shop.

Major Update Packs are only available from 9:00 am on Friday, September 30 until 11:59 pm on Tuesday, October 4, so don't miss out!

School Idol Starter Packs are also available. For the cost of 5 Love Gems, you get a lot of extra items!

■Pack on sale

Scout 11 Scouting Ticket x1

SR Alpaca x5
Available until: 11:59 pm on Tuesday, October 4

Major Update Pack: Aqours
Scout 11 Scouting Ticket x1
SR or above Aqours Scouting Ticket x1
SR Alpaca x5
Available until: 11:59 pm on Tuesday, October 4

School Idol Starter Pack: μ's
SR or above μ's Scouting Ticket x1
Love Gems x5

100,000G

Love Gems x5

100,000G

■Item details

- Scout 11 Scouting Ticket
A ticket that can be used for Scout 11 Honor Student Scouting in either μ's or Aqours mode.
Use of this ticket is prioritized over other tickets and Love Gems.
You will receive Honor Student Bonus by using this ticket.

- SR or above μ's Scouting Ticket
A ticket that can be used for "SR or above μ's Scouting."
Features SR or above μ's members that were available from Honor Student Scouting since May 2014.
See Details of the "SR or above μ's Scouting" for more details on which members appear.
"SR or above μ's Scouting" becomes available in Home screen and μ's Scouting menu only when you hold this ticket.
You will not receive Honor Student Bonus by using this ticket.

- SR or above Aqours Scouting Ticket
A ticket that can be used for "SR or above Aqours Scouting."
Features SR or above Aqours members that were available from Honor Student Scouting since September 2016.
See HERE for more details on which members appear.
"SR or above Aqours Scouting" becomes available in Home screen and Aqours Scouting menu only when you hold this ticket.
You will not receive Honor Student Bonus by using this ticket.

Note
Each pack can only be purchased once per person with currency payment. Love Gems cannot be used.
See Details of the "SR or above Aqours Scouting" for more details on which members appear.
Scout 11 Scouting Ticket, SR or above μ's Scouting Ticket, SR or above Aqours Scouting Ticket, are all sent directly to your inventory.

We hope you enjoy the special packs, and thank you for playing School Idol Festival!
