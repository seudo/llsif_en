New Event Notice

Date 2015-05-04 一 Tags POST
This round of the Score Match will run from 9:00 am on Tuesday, May 5 to 8:00 am on Friday, May 15 UTC.

Four Player Face-off Match
Choose a difficultly level, and you will be automatically matched with up to three other players. A random Live Show will be selected, and all players will start the Live Show at the same time. At the end of the Live Show, you will be ranked according to your score, and you will receive Event Points based on the difficulty level and your rank.

Note: You will not receive Event Points for Live Shows completed after 8:00 am on Friday, May 15 UTC, even if you start before this time.

You can easily communicate with the other players by using set phrases while you are being matched, and when the results are displayed.

Rewards
Umi Sonoda [Samurai] (SR)
Nanako Yamauchi [Teacher] (R)
Alpaca (R)
and more

Note: Umi Sonoda [Samurai] (SR) is being released early for this event, and will be added as an Honor Student in late-May. SR Club Members released early for events have a lower appearance rate from Scouting than standard SR Members. The Alpacas can be Removed in exchange for G or used for Practicing, and the Teachers are ideal for Practicing, but neither can be added to a Team or be Idolized.

Rewards depend on:
- The total number of Event Points you accumulate.
- Your ranking based on your total Event Points.

Rewards will be distributed after the event has finished.

Song Selections:
- Selection 1
From 9:00 am on Tuesday, May 5 to 7:59 am on Friday, May 8.
8 songs each on EASY, NORMAL, HARD, and 5 songs on EXPERT.

- Selection 2
From 8:00 am on Friday, May 8 to 7:59 am on Monday, May 11.
Fully replace Selection 1 with 8 different songs each on EASY, NORMAL, HARD, and 5 different songs on EXPERT.

- Selection 3
From 8:00 am on Monday, May 11 to 8:00 am on Friday, May 15.
Selection 1 + 2 all together!
