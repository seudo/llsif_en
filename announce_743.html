Upcoming New Event

Date 2016-07-28 四 Tags POST
This round of the Score Match will run from 9:00 am on Friday, July 29 to 8:00 am on Monday, August 8 UTC.

Four Player Face-off Match
Choose a difficultly level, and you will be automatically matched with up to three other players.
If there aren't enough actual players, an AI controlled player will be added.
A random Live Show will be selected, and all players will start the Live Show at the same time.
At the end of the Live Show, you will be ranked according to your score, and you will receive Event Points based on the difficulty level and your rank.
You can communicate with the other players by using set phrases while you are being matched, and when the results are displayed.

Note:
- You will not receive Event Points for Live Shows completed after 8:00 am on Monday, August 8 UTC, even if you start before this time.
- If the game enters maintenance during a Live Show, you will not receive Exp, G, and any other rewards you might have received.
- You cannot choose to continue a Score Match Live Show.
- When playing the same difficulty level one after another, you are less likely to be assigned the same song consecutively.

What is the "TECHNICAL" difficulty level?
Only the hardest of the Expert songs are selected. Required LP and the rewards you could gain are the same as the EXPERT level.

Rewards
The rewards depend on the total number of Event Points you accumulate, and your final ranking based on your total Event Points.

SR Maki Nishikino [Rock!]
SR Umi Sonoda [Explorer]
R Kyoko Sasahara [Teacher]
R Nanako Yamauchi [Teacher]
and more!

Note:
- SR Maki Nishikino [Rock!] and SR Umi Sonoda [Explorer] are being released early for this event, and will be added to Honor Student Scouting and SR/UR Scouting during August.
- SR Club Members released early for events have a lower appearance rate from Scouting than standard SR Members.
- The Teachers are ideal for Practicing, but can't be added to a Team or be Idolized.
- The number of people who will win the reward is the same as the Medley Festival Round 6.
- Rewards will be distributed after the event has finished.

Song Selections
Selection 1
From 9:00 am on Friday, July 29 to 7:59 am on Monday, August 1.
12 songs each on EASY, NORMAL, HARD, 10 songs on EXPERT, 12 songs on TECHNICAL.

Selection 2
From 8:00 am on Monday, August 1 to 7:59 am on Thursday, August 4.
Fully replace Selection 1 with 12 different songs each on EASY, NORMAL, HARD, 10 different songs on EXPERT, and 12 different songs on TECHNICAL.

Selection 3
From 8:00 am on Thursday, August 4 to 8:00 am on Monday, August 8.
Selection 1 + 2 all together!

The following songs are released on TECHNICAL.
- A selection of Daily Expert songs (Stage Level ★11)
- Random Expert songs (Stage Level ★9 or above)

Note:
- The pattern of Rhythm Icons are totally randomized for Random EXPERT songs. However, matched players will all see the same set of randomized Rhythm Icons. - The songs available for Random EXPERT are the same as EXPERT songs.
- Random EXPERT songs may include songs that are being released on EXPERT for the first time.


We hope you continue to enjoy Love Live! School Idol Festival.
