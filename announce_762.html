Upcoming Event

Date 2016-08-11 四 Tags POST
Event Details -
This event will run from 9:00 am on Friday, August 12 to 8:00 am on Monday, August 22 UTC.
During the event, special Ribbons tokens inside rhythm icons will appear in all Live Shows. Tap these icons to collect them and also get Event Points. Use the tokens you collect to play the event song, which will give you more Event Points compared to other songs.

Note:
- The amount of Event Points you can get from the event song depends on your Score and Combo Rank.
- You will not receive Event Points for Live Shows completed after 8:00 am on Monday, August 22 UTC, even if you start before this time. There is no event story to go with this event.
- We plan to add the event song as a Hit song sometime after the event has finished.
- There is no event story to go with this event.

Event Achievement Rewards -
SR Chika Takami [Rain Season]
R Satoko Miyama [Teacher]
and more.

Event Ranking Rewards -
SR Riko Sakurauchi [Rain Season]
R Kyoko Sasahara [Teacher]
and more.

Note:
- SR Chika Takami [Rain Season] and SR Riko Sakurauchi [Rain Season] are being released early for this event, and they do not have voice audios associated with them. Voice will be added later when they are officially added to Scouting at a later date.
- The Side Stories you unlock by maxing the Bond with Idolized versions of these members can be read from Story > Side Stories > Others.
- SR Members released early for events have a lower appearance rate from Scouting than standard SR Members.
- Teachers are ideal for Practicing, but can't be added to a Team or be Idolized.

Reward Conditions
With the addition of rewards SRs, we have made some changes to the rewards.

- Achievement Rewards
Changed some rewards and also changed the amount of Event Pts. required for some rewards.

- Ranking Rewards
Changed some rewards.

- Song Ranking Rewards
Changed some rewards. Certain ranks will give same rewards.

Note:
- We have made adjustments to the number of people who will be eligible for the rewards. Please see the in-game event rewards section for more details.
- To coincide with the changes, the amount of Event Pts. received from Events Song is also adjusted.
- Rewards will be distributed after the event has finished.

The event tokens required for the event song:
EASY - 15
NORMAL - 30
HARD - 45
EXPERT/RANDOM EXPERT - 75

X4 Event Song
From 12:00 am on Thursday, August 18 until the end of the event.
Four times the amount of tokens are required to play these songs, but you will also receive four times the Event Pts.

The Bubbles required for the X4 song:
EASY - 60
NORMAL - 120
HARD - 180
EXPERT/RANDOM EXPERT - 300

Note:
- The notes are randomized for Random Expert event songs.
- Only Expert event songs are available on Random.
- Live rewards such as EXP, G, number of Members, and Member levels are the same as for regular event songs.

Extra Bonus Gs
From 12:00 am on Monday, August 15 until the end of the event.
Earn extra Gs from all regular Hits and B-Sides except for the event song.

Hits EASY - 300%
Hits NORMAL - 500%
Hits HARD - 1000%
B-Sides EXPERT - 1000%

We hope you continue to enjoy Love Live! School Idol Festival.
