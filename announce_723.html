New Club Members Added

Date 2016-07-11 一 Tags POST
We have added new members with the maintenance that started at 8:00 am on Monday, July 11 UTC.

μ's members are on a stealth mission dressed up as female ninja "Kunoichi"! Idolize them into also ninja themed stage costumes. Don't forget to check out the brand new side stories too!

In addition there are also nine new R members both in Honor and Regular Student Scouting. Idolize them to change them from school club activity clothes to "Dancing Stars on Me!" stage costumes!

These newly members have increased appearance rates from the Honor Student Scouting until 8:00 am on Friday, July 15 UTC.
(Some exceptions apply, please see the Note below)
