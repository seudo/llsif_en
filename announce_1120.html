New μ's Members Added

Date 2017-01-16 一 Tags POST
We have added new μ's members at 9:00 am on Monday, January 16 UTC.

Take a glimpse at their regular school life in this series with μ's members in their summer uniforms. Idolize them into super cute school idol costumes! Don't miss out on their side stories where each of them reflect on their school idolhood.

- Newly added members to μ's Honor Student Scouting:
UR Honoka Kosaka [Idol Costume]
SR Rin Hoshizora [Idol Costume]
SR Maki Nishikino [Idol Costume]
SR Nico Yazawa [Idol Costume]

These newly added members have increased appearance rates from the μ's Honor Student Scouting until 8:00 am on Friday, January 20 UTC. Also, "Scout 11" on Honor Student Scouting guarantees an SR or above member!

Note:
- Individual member appearance rates are equal among each rarity except for the following:

[Appearance rate increased members in Honor Student Scouting]
Regardless of the total number of members available, these members have a flat rate increase in appearance rate per member within each rarity.

- The newly added members to Honor Student Scouting are also available from SR/UR Scouting (no SSR) , SR or above μ's Scouting, and SSR or above μ's Scouting.
- Increased appearance rates of the new members does not apply to SR/UR Scouting, SR or above μ's Scouting, and SSR or above μ's Scouting.

Please check the Details found on the scouting page for more information.
