Birthday Special Scouting

Date 2017-08-02 三 Tags REMOVE
We will be running a "Step-Up Scouting" as well as a "Solo Scouting" dedicated to Honoka Kosaka to celebrate her birthday! These scouting are available for three days only.

Available Period
From 12:00 am on Wed, August 2 until 11:59 pm on Fri, August 4 UTC.

■ Honoka Kosaka Step-Up Scouting
Step-Up Scouting allows you to Scout 11 for less Love Gems on the first and the second Scout 11s with an SR or above guaranteed, and on the third Scout 11 it guarantees an SSR or above!

Step 1: Scout 11 for 30 Love Gems with an SR or above guaranteed.
Step 2: Scout 11 for 40 Love Gems with an SR or above guaranteed.
Step 3: Scout 11 for 50 Love Gems with an SSR or above guaranteed.

■ Honoka Kosaka Solo Scouting
Special Honor Student Scouting featuring Honoka Kosaka only! Scout 1 or Scout 11 using Love Gems. Scout 11 will guarantee at least an SR or above!

Notes
- Step-Up Scouting will end and no longer be visible after you perform Step 3.
- These special scouts feature versions of Honoka Kosaka that were available from μ's Honor Student Scouting from May 2014 to June 28, 2017 UTC.
- Appearance rates by rarity are fixed per scout type and do not change.
- Individual member appearance rates are equal within each rarity except for event released members.
- Event released members have their appearance rates reduced to one-fifth.
- See the "Details" section of each scouting for more info on which members appear.
- Honoka Kosaka Step-Up Scouting and Honoka Kosaka Solo Scouting are both eligible for Honor Student Bonus.

We hope you continue to enjoy Love Live! School Idol Festival.
