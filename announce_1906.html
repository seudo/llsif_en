School Idol Fan Packs

Date 2017-09-01 五 Tags POST
These special packs are only available at the beginning of the month. You can purchase them by tapping the banners in the Shop.

Availability Period
From 12:00 am on Fri, September 1 until 11:59 pm on Wed, September 6 UTC.

■ Available Packs
・School Idol Fan Pack: μ's
・School Idol Fan Pack: Aqours

Each pack contains a "Scout 11 Scouting Ticket" and an "SR or above Scouting" Ticket.

■ Item details
・Scout 11 Scouting Ticket
A ticket for a Scout 11 scouting.
You will receive Honor Student Bonus by using this ticket.

・SR or above Scouting Ticket
A ticket for a single scouting on SR or above scouting.
You will NOT receive Honor Student Bonus by using this ticket.

- μ's version
Features SR or above μ's members that have been available from Honor Student Scouting since May 2014.

- Aqours version
Features SR or above Aqours members that have been available from Honor Student Scouting since September 2016.

Notes
- Each pack can only be purchased once per person during the available period.
- They can only be purchased via payment methods accepted by respective application stores. Love Gems cannot be used.
- Scout 11 Scouting Tickets and SR or above Scouting Tickets, will all be sent directly to your inventory and not to your Present Box.
- The ticket specific scouting will become available only when you hold an eligible ticket.
- See the "Details" section of each scouting for more info on which members appear.

We hope you enjoy the special packs, and thank you for playing School Idol Festival!
