New μ's Members Added

Date 2017-06-11 日 Tags POST
We added new μ's members at 9:00 am on Sunday, June 11 UTC.

The μ's members are looking at accessories with their birth stones in this series. Idolize them into birth stone themed costumes! Don't forget to check out their side stories to find out what their birth stones mean to them♪

■ Members newly added to μ's Honor Student Scouting:
UR Nozomi Tojo [Birth Stones]
SR Kotori Minami [Birth Stones]

These newly added members have increased appearance rates from the μ's Honor Student Scouting until 7:59 am on Friday, June 16 UTC. Also don't forget, "Scout 11" guarantees an SR or above member!

In addition, the following previously removed members were added back to μ's Honor Student Scouting. Their appearance rates are not increased.

UR Maki Nishikino "Into Legend"
UR Rin Hoshizora "Twinkling Stars"
SR Honoka Kosaka "All Together Now"
SR Kotori Minami "Vocal Vibration"
SR Nozomi Tojo "Joyful Twins"
SR Hanayo Koizumi "I'm Moved!"
SR Eli Ayase "Justice Is Blind"
SR Umi Sonoda "Night Sky"
SR Nico Yazawa "Nico Yazawa's Shine"

Notes
- There are no event released members with this update.
- Appearance rates by rarity are fixed per scout type and do not change.
- Individual member appearance rates are equal among each rarity except for the following:

[Appearance rate increased members in Honor Student Scouting]
Regardless of the total number of members available, these members have a flat rate increase in appearance rate per member within each rarity.

[Event released members]
Each member's appearance rate is reduced to one-fifth.

- The newly added members to Honor Student Scouting are also available from SR/UR Scouting, SR or above μ's Scouting, and SSR or above μ's Scouting.
- Increased appearance rates of the new members does not apply to SR/UR Scouting, SR or above μ's Scouting, and SSR or above μ's Scouting.
- See the "Details" section of each scouting for more info on which members appear.

■ Members removed
The following members were removed from the Honor Student Scouting.

UR (total of 1)
Name Attribute
【Live Show Prep】 Maki NishikinoSmile

SR (total of 1)
Name Attribute
【My Taisho Era Self】 Nozomi Tojo Smile

Notes
- The members that are being removed from the Honor Student Scouting are still available from Limited Scouting, SR/UR Scouting, SR or above μ's Scouting, and SSR or above μ's Scouting.
- We plan to continue making changes to the Honor Student Scouting lineup regularly.

We hope you continue to enjoy Love Live! School Idol Festival.
