lily white Scouting

Date 2017-08-10 四 Tags POST
Team Scouting that features lily white members only is available now. There is also a specific series daily with an increased appearance rate! Scout 11 on the lily white Team Scouting will also guarantee an SR or above member.

Availability period
From 12:00 am on Thu, August 10 until 11:59 pm on Sat, August 12 UTC.

■ Thursday, August 10 UTC
Increased chance for the Circus series.

UR Umi Sonoda "I Won't Disappoint!"
Leader Skill: Smile Empress
Receive Smile Points bonus equivalent to 12% of Cool Points
+ additional 6% Smile Points bonus to lily white members.

SR Rin Hoshizora "I Feel So Light!"
SR Nozomi Tojo "Mysterious Stage"

■ Friday, August 11 UTC
Increased chance for the Dancer series.

UR Rin Hoshizora "Respect!"
Leader Skill: Pure Princess
Receive Pure Points bonus equivalent to 12% of Smile Points
+ additional 6% Pure Points bonus to first year members.

SR Umi Sonoda "Increased Exposure?!"
SR Nozomi Tojo "Dancing Psychic"

■ Saturday, August 12 UTC
Increased chance for the Dance Party series.

UR Nozomi Tojo "Secret Feelings"
Leader Skill: Cool Angel
Receive Cool Points bonus equivalent to 12% of Pure Points
+ additional 6% Cool Points bonus to lily white members.

SR Umi Sonoda "World History"
SR Rin Hoshizora "Light Steps"

Notes
- Individual member appearance rates are equal among each rarity except for 1. appearance rate increased members with a flat rate increase in appearance rate per member within each rarity regardless of the total number of members available, and 2. event released members, with each member's appearance rate reduced to one-fifth.
- Due to change in the total number of members available, the individual appearance rates per member have changed.
- These scouts do not contain the new members that are scheduled to be added on Monday, August 14, 2017 UTC.
- Increased chance promotion for newly added Aqours members in Honor Student Scouting has ended on Wednesday, August 9, 2017 UTC.
- See the "Details" section of each scouting for more info on which members appear.

We hope you continue to enjoy Love Live! School Idol Festival.
