30 Million Players Worldwide Celebration Event

Date 2016-10-13 四 Tags POST
Thank you for playing Love Live! School Idol Festival.
We are very pleased to announce that we now have over 30 million players worldwide!
To celebrate the occasion, we will be running a special event from Saturday, October 15 UTC.

Round 1
Players that log in to the game from Saturday, October 15 to Sunday, October 23 UTC will receive a Love Gem or a Scouting Ticket as a special login bonus.

Round 2
30 Million players worldwide celebration packages introduced.

Package Details:
30 Million Players Pack: μ's
- Scout 11 Scouting Ticket x 1
- SSR or above μ's Scouting Ticket x 1
- 1,000,000 G

30 Million Players Pack: Aqours
- Scout 11 Scouting Ticket x 1
- SSR or above Aqours Scouting Ticket x 1
- 1,000,000 G

Availability Period:
12:00 am on Saturday, October 15 to 11:59 pm on Monday, October 24 UTC.
- Limited to one of each purchase per player.

Round 3
All nine members of Aqours added as new R club members.

A total of nine new R members that can be Idolized from their practice outfits to “Young Dreamer” stage outfits will be added to Honor Scouting and Regular Scouting. Exact release dates will be announced at a later date.

Newly Added Members:
R Chika Takami
R Riko Sakurauchi
R Kanan Matsuura
R Dia Kurosawa
R You Watanabe
R Yoshiko Tsushima
R Hanamaru Kunikida
R Mari Ohara
R Ruby Kurosawa

Round 4
A very special club member to be distributed as a login bonus.

The winning illustration for the μ's Fan Meeting Tour 2015 - Love Live! in Your Town! contest held from May 30 to August 9, 2015 in Japan was a stunning illustration featuring Nico Yazawa. A special member bearing this illustration will be given out as a login bonus. Distribution dates will be announced at a later date.

Round 5
New ranking system for Events to be introduced.

In addition to the traditional cumulative point ranking system, a new form of score keeping will be added to the ranking system for events. Festival events will feature a new score ranking, and Score Match events will feature a special ranking. These events will be backed by some fabulous prizes.

No new ranking system will be added to Token Collection Events. However, rewards for Score Rankings will be revamped, and the rewards that have been distributed up until now will be included in the overall cumulative points ranking system. Exact release dates will be announced at a later date.

Round 6
First ever Aqours and Love Live! School Idol Festival collaboration single announced!

The first ever Aqours song created in collaboration with Love Live! School Idol Festival will be released on Wednesday, November 23, 2016. This Christmas-themed single has been much-anticipated by fans of the series for some time now. As a special treat for fans, an exclusive side-story will be included in the recording. The single will also be added to the game as a special time-limited song. Exact availability period will be announced at a later date.

We hope you continue to enjoy Love Live! School Idol Festival.
2016/10/13
