Coupon Scouting UpdateCoupon Scouting Update2017/07/10

Date 2017-07-10 一 Tags POST
We have made the following updates to the Coupon Scouting with the update on Monday, July 10 UTC. With this update, SR/UR, SSR/UR and UR Scouting are now available in both μ's and Aqours modes.

1. SSR/UR and UR Scouting added.

SSR/UR Scouting
Features SSR and UR members only.
10 Scouting Coupons required.
Draw ratio by rarity: 80% SSR, 20% UR

UR Scouting
Features UR members only.
25 Scouting Coupons required.
Draw ratio by rarity: 100% UR

2. Aqours Coupon Scouting introduced.
Aqours SR/UR, SSR/UR and UR Coupon Scouting are now available.

Notes
- Supporting Member Scouting is not yet available in Aqours mode.
- Each μ's mode Coupon Scouting features members of the corresponding rarity that were available from Honor Student Scouting since May, 2014 (excluding Supporting Member Scouting.)
- Each Aqours mode Coupon Scouting features members of the corresponding rarity that were available from Honor Student Scouting since September, 2016.
- Appearance rates by rarity are fixed per scout type and do not change.
- Individual member appearance rates are equal within each rarity except for event released members.
- Event released members have their appearance rates reduced to one-fifth.
- Member lineup and the total number of members available will change when new members are added to Honor Student Scouting. Because of this, the individual appearance rates per member will also change.
- See the "Details" section of each scouting for more info on which members appear.

We hope you continue to enjoy Love Live! School Idol Festival.
