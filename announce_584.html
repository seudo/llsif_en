Upcoming New Event

Date 2016-03-03 四 Tags POST
EVENT DETAILS

This event will run from 9:00 am on Friday, March 4 to 8:00 am on Monday, March 14 UTC.

During the event, special "Ball" rhythm icons will appear in all Live Shows. Tap these icons to get Cups and Event Points. Use the Balls to play the B-Side “FUTARI HAPPINESS”, where you can get more than the normal amount of Event Points.

The amount of Event Points you can get in “FUTARI HAPPINESS” depends on your Score and Combo Rank.
We plan to add “FUTARI HAPPINESS” as a Hit song sometime after the event has finished.

You will not receive Event Points for Live Shows completed after 8:00 am on Monday, March 14 UTC, even if you start before this time. There is no event story to go with this event.


Rin Hoshizora [Playing Catch] (SR)
Satoko Miyama [Teacher] (R)
and many more!

Note:
- Rin Hoshizora [Playing Catch] (SR) is being released early for this event, and will be added as an Honor Student during March.
- SR Members released early for events have a lower appearance rate from Scouting than standard SR Members.
- Teachers are ideal for Practicing, but can't be added to a Team or be Idolized.


The number of people who are eligible for rewards may vary depending on the total number of participants.
Rewards will be distributed after the event, and what you receive will depend on the following:

・The total number of Event Points you accumulate.
・Your Event Ranking.
・Your "FUTARI HAPPINESS" Song Ranking.


We are also running the following specials from 12:00 am on March 11 UTC until the end of the event.

・x4 Event Songs
Four times the amount of Balls are required to play these songs, but you will also receive four times the Event Pts.
Live rewards such as EXP, G, number of Members, and Member levels are the same as for regular event songs.

・Expert Event Song
A higher difficulty version of the event song is available under B-Sides, Expert.
75 Balls required.

・Random Notes Expert Event Song
The same Expert Event Song as above, but the Notes are randomized!

Note:
- Playing any of the three song versions above will not count towards the Event Song Ranking.

・Extra Bonus G
You can earn extra G from certain songs: 150% from Normal, 200% from Hard, and 300% from Expert.
Eligible songs change every day.

We hope you continue to enjoy Love Live! School Idol Festival.
