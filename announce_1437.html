Third Anniversary Login Bonus

Date 2017-05-04 四 Tags POST
To celebrate this momentous occasion, we are running the Third Anniversary Countdown Login Bonus! Don't miss out on this great opportunity to receive lots of fantastic bonuses!

Scouting Coupon - Collect five Scouting Coupons to perform SR/UR Scouting which has a greater chance for an UR member.

Special Login Bonus Period:
From 12:00 am on Thu, May 4 until 11:59 pm on Fri, May 12 UTC.

Special Login Bonuses:
First log in - Love Gem x 1
Second log in - Scouting Ticket x 1
Third log in - SR Yoko Sato [Teacher] x 1
Fourth log in - Scouting Ticket x 1
Fifth log in - Love Gem x 1
Sixth log in - Scouting Ticket x 1
Seventh log in - Love Gem x 1
Eighth log in - N Stickers x 20

We hope you continue to enjoy Love Live! School Idol Festival.
