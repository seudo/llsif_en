New Club Members Added

Date 2016-09-21 三 Tags REMOVE
We have added new members with the maintenance that started at 8:00 am on Wednesday, September 21 UTC.

The members of μ's are donning their kimono and hakama for the "Taisho Romanticism" themed event at a cafe. Idolize them into frilly hakama stage costumes. Don't forget to check out the brand new side stories too!

Taisho Romanticism aka Taisho Roman, is a style of fashion that originates from the Taisho era in Japan from 1912 to 1926, during which Western culture continued to influence the daily lives of the Japanese people. The style is characterized by the combination of traditional kimono with Western elements such as hats, gloves, heels, and accessories.
