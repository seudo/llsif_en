Additional info on Ver. 2.0.5 update

With the update to Ver. 2.0.5 which was carried out on December 4, 2014, there were some feature changes that were not mentioned in the original update notification.Please see the following for additional information.Removed the following entries from the "Reward History" in order to reduce server connection errors.- Members received as Live Performance completion rewards.- Love Gems, Gs, and Friend Pts. received from song goals.- Friend Pts. received from guests.- Love Gems received from Main Stories.- Love Gems received from Side Stories.NoteOnly the entries in the history are being removed, the actual items remain in your inventory.The following will continue to be displayed.- Members, Love Gems, Gs, Friend Pts. and Scouting Tickets received from Presents.- Members received from Scouring.- Album rewards- Event rewards.We apologize for any inconvenience this may have caused, and thank you for your understanding.We hope you continue to enjoy Love Live! School Idol Festival.October 22, 20152015/10/22
