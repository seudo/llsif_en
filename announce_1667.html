Welcome Summer Campaign

Date 2017-07-01 六 Tags POST
To celebrate the coming of summer, the global version of Love Live! School Idol Festival will run an exclusive campaign packed full of fun!

1. Welcome Summer Special Login Bonus
Log in to the game during the campaign period to get a special login bonus for every day you log in up to a maximum of nine days. Don't miss out on this great opportunity to receive extra Love Gems and Scouting Tickets!

Special Login Bonus Availability
From 12:00 am on Sat, July 1 until 11:59 pm on Thu, July 20 UTC.

Special Login Bonus Details
First log in - 1 Love Gem
Second log in - 1 Love Gem
Third log in - 1 Scouting Ticket
Fourth log in - 1 Love Gem
Fifth log in - 1 Love Gem
Sixth log in - 1 Love Gem
Seventh log in - 1 Love Gem
Eighth log in - 1 Love Gem
Ninth log in - 1 Scouting Ticket

2. Welcome Summer Special Goals
Special Goals will be available for you to try during the period as indicated below. Achieve these special Goals to receive Love Gems and a Scouting Coupon as rewards.

Special Goals Availability
From 9:00 am on Mon, July 3 until 7:59 am on Wed, July 19 UTC.

Welcome Summer Goal 1:
Complete "MIRAI TICKET" once on Easy during the event.
Welcome Summer Goal 2:
Complete "MIRAI TICKET" once on Normal during the event.
Welcome Summer Goal 3:
Complete "MIRAI TICKET" once on Expert during the event.
Welcome Summer Goal 4:
Complete "Natsu Owaranaide." once on Master during the event.
Welcome Summer Goal 5:
Achieve "Full Combo" 75 times by the end of the event.

Notes
- Full Combo count you already have prior to this event will also count toward Welcome Summer Goal 5.
- Please view the "Goals" section of the game for more information on progress and rewards.
- The Aqours song "MIRAI TICKET" is unlocked by clearing the Aqours Main Story Chapter 10, Story 1.
- The number of Full Combos required for "Welcome Summer Goal 5" must be achieved while the Special Goals are available.
- Welcome Summer Goals will no longer be achievable when the Special Goals period is over.

Thank you for playing Love Live! School Idol Festival, and we hope you continue to enjoy the game!
