Upcoming New Event

Date 2017-01-11 三 Tags POST
Event Details

This event will run from 9:00 am on Thursday, January 12 to 8:00 am on Friday, January 20 UTC.

During the event, special tokens inside rhythm icons will appear in all Live Shows. Tap these icons to collect the tokens and get some Event Points. Use the tokens you collect to play the event song, which will give you much more Event Points compared to other songs. Depending on how much Event Points you collect, you can win various great prizes!

Note:
- The amount of Event Points you can get from the event song depends on your Score and Combo Rank.
- You will not receive Event Points for Live Shows completed after 8:00 am on Friday, January 20 UTC, even if you start before this time.
- There is no event story to go with this event.

Event Achievement Rewards
SR Rin Hoshizora [Shrine Maiden]
R Nanako Yamauchi [Teacher]
and many more!

Note:
- SR Rin Hoshizora [Shrine Maiden] is being released early for this event, and will be added to Honor Student scouting, SR/UR scouting, and SR or above μ's Scouting during February.
- SR Members released early for events have a lower appearance rate from Scouting than standard SR Members.
- Teachers are ideal for Practicing, but can't be added to a Team or be Idolized.

Rewards depend on:
- The total number of Event Points you accumulate.
- Your ranking based on your total Event Points.
- Your Event Song ranking.

Note:
- For more details on achievement rewards and ranking rewards, please see the in-game event rewards section.
- Ranking rewards will be distributed after the event has finished.

The event tokens required to play the event song:
EASY - 15
NORMAL - 30
HARD - 45
EXPERT/RANDOM EXPERT - 75

Note:
- Only Expert event songs are available on Random.
- X4 songs, Expert songs and Random Expert songs are not eligible for the Event Song Ranking.
- The notes are randomized for Random Expert event songs.

X4 Event Songs
From 12:00 am on Monday, January 16 until the end of the event.
Four times the amount of tokens are required to play these songs, but you will also receive four times the Event Points.

The event tokens required to play the X4 song:
EASY - 60
NORMAL - 120
HARD - 180
EXPERT/RANDOM EXPERT - 300

Extra Bonus Gs
From 12:00 am on Tuesday, January 17 until the end of the event.
Earn extra Gs from certain songs.

Hits NORMAL - 150%
Hits HARD - 200%
Hits EXPERT - 300%
B-Sides EXPERT - 300%
B-Sides MASTER - 300%

We hope you continue to enjoy Love Live! School Idol Festival.
