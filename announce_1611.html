Attribute Scouting

Date 2017-06-30 五 Tags POST
Attribute Scouting with a different Attribute featured each day will be available from 12:00 am on June 30 to 7:59 am on Monday, July 3 UTC. Scout 11 on the Attribute Scouting guarantees an SR or above member! Also, there will be a daily selection of specific series with increased appearance rates!

Available Periods
Pure Attribute Scouting - 12:00 am to 11:59 pm on Friday, June 30 UTC
Cool Attribute Scouting - 12:00 am to 11:59 pm on Saturday, July 1 UTC
Smile Attribute Scouting - 12:00 am on Sunday, July 2 to 7:59 am on Monday, July 3 UTC

■ 12:00 am to 11:59 pm on Friday, June 30 UTC
Pure Attribute Scouting with increased chance for the Zodiac series!

UR Maki Nishikino "Into Legend"
Leader Skill: Pure Angel
Receive 9% Pure Points bonus
+ additional 3% Pure Points bonus to μ's members.

SR Honoka Kosaka "All Together Now"
SR Eli Ayase "Justice Is Blind"

■ 12:00 am to 11:59 pm on Saturday, July 1 UTC
Cool Attribute Scouting with increased chance for the Cyber series!

UR Honoka Kosaka "Come Play With Nico!"
Leader Skill: Cool Empress
Receive 9% Cool Points bonus
+ additional 3% Cool Points bonus to μ's members.

SR Rin Hoshizora "All to Myself!"
SR Hanayo Koizumi "Focus on the Movie!"

■ 12:00 am to 11:59 pm on Sunday, July 2 UTC
Smile Attribute Scouting with increased chance for the Fairy Tale series!

UR Kotori Minami "Little Princess"
Leader Skill: Smile Angel
Receive Smile Points bonus equivalent to 12% of Pure Points
+ additional 6% Smile Points bonus to Printemps members.
　
SR Umi Sonoda "Umi Returns the Favor"
SR Nico Yazawa "Please Don't Eat Me"

■ 12:00 am to 7:59 am on Monday, July 3 UTC
Smile Attribute Scouting with increased chance for the Taisho Roman series!

UR Hanayo Koizumi "What Era Is It?"
Leader Skill: Smile Angel
Receive Smile Points bonus equivalent to 12% of Pure Points
+ additional 6% Smile Points bonus to first year members.

SR Umi Sonoda "Does This Suit Me?"
SR Nozomi Tojo "My Taisho Era Self"

Notes
- Due to change in the total number of members available, the individual appearance rates per member have changed.
- Individual member appearance rates are equal among each rarity except for the following:

[Appearance rate increased members]
Regardless of the total number of members available, these members have a flat rate increase in appearance rate per member within each rarity.

[Event released members]
Each member's appearance rate is reduced to one-fifth.

- See the "Details" section of each scouting for more info on which members appear.
- These scouts do not contain the new members that are scheduled to be added on Wednesday, June 28, 2017 UTC.

We hope you continue to enjoy Love Live! School Idol Festival.
