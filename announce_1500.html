Third Anniversary Celebration!

Date 2017-05-25 四 Tags POST
Originally launched on May 12, 2014, the global version of Love Live! School Idol Festival will celebrate the third anniversary of its release in style starting on Thursday, May 25.

1. Third Anniversary Login Bonus
2. Third Anniversary Special Packs
3. Login Bonus UR in Sticker Shop

1. Third Anniversary Login Bonus
Log in to the game once a day during the nine day campaign period to receive special login bonuses including a Scouting Coupon. Don't miss out on this great opportunity to receive lots of fantastic bonuses!

Scouting Coupon - Collect five Scouting Coupons to perform SR/UR Scouting which has a greater chance for a UR member.

Special Login Bonus Period
From 12:00 am on Thu, May 25 until 11:59 pm on Fri, Jun 2 UTC.

Special Login Bonus Details
First log in - 1 Love Gem
Second log in - 400,000 Gs
Third log in - 1 Love Gem
Fourth log in - 40,000 Friend Pts
Fifth log in - 1 Scouting Ticket
Sixth log in - 1 Love Gem
Seventh log in - 1 SR Yoko Sato [Teacher]
Eighth log in - 20 N Stickers
Ninth log in - 1 Scouting Coupon

2. Third Anniversary Special Packs
Special packs filled with great items will be on sale for a limited period of five days.

Availability Period
From 12:00 am on Thu, May 25 until 11:59 pm on Mon, May 29 UTC.

Available Packs
・3rd Anniv. Starter Pack: μ's
Love Gems x5
SR or above μ's Scouting Ticket x1
R Alpaca x3

・3rd Anniv. Starter Pack: Aqours
Love Gems x5
SR or above Aqours Scouting Ticket x1
R Shiitake x3

・3rd Anniv. Fan Pack: μ's
Scout 11 Scouting Ticket x1
SSR or above μ's Scouting Ticket x1
R Alpaca x5

・3rd Anniv. Fan Pack: Aqours
Scout 11 Scouting Ticket x1
SSR or above Aqours Scouting Ticket x1
R Shiitake x5

Item details
・SR or above Scouting Ticket
A ticket for one scout on SR or above scouting.
You will NOT receive Honor Student Bonus by using this ticket.

- μ's version
Features SR or above μ's members that have been available from Honor Student Scouting since May 2014.

- Aqours version
Features SR or above Aqours members that have been available from Honor Student Scouting since September 2016.

・Scout 11 Scouting Ticket
A ticket for a Scout 11 scouting.
You will receive Honor Student Bonus by using this ticket.

・SSR or above Scouting Ticket
A ticket for one scout on SSR or above Scouting.

- μ's version
Features SSR or above μ's members that were available from Honor Student Scouting since May 2014 until May 2017.

- Aqours version
Features SSR or above Aqours members that have been available from Honor Student Scouting since September 2016 until May 2017.

Notes
- Each pack can only be purchased once per person during the available period.
- They can only be purchased via payment methods accepted by respective application stores. Love Gems cannot be used.
- The ticket specific scouting will become available only when you hold an eligible ticket.
- Scout 11 Scouting Tickets, SR or above Scouting Tickets, and SSR or above Scouting Tickets will all be sent directly to your inventory and not to your Present Box.
- See the "Details" section of each scouting for more info on which members appear.

3. Login Bonus UR in Sticker Shop
Some of the UR members released as login bonuses in the past will be available in the Sticker Shop.

Availability Period
From 12:00 am on Thu, May 25 until 11:59 pm on Tue, Jun 6 UTC.

UR members added
Kotori Minami “Snowman, Kotori Style”
Eli Ayase “Together at the Beach”
Maki Nishikino “Christmas Night”
Nozomi Tojo “Try Your Luck”
Hanayo Koizumi “μ's Cherries!”

Thank you for playing Love Live! School Idol Festival, and we hope you continue to enjoy the game!
