1st Anniversary Event

Date 2015-05-12 二 Tags POST
May 12 is the 1st anniversary for Love Live! School Idol Festival!
Thank you so much for playing the game, and for all your support.

In order to express our gratitude, and to celebrate this occasion, we are running a series of special events starting today.


A new Daily Special song a day will be available to play starting Tuesday May 12.
There are 6 new songs in total, and these will be available in addition to the regular Daily Special songs.

Furthermore, EXPERT mode will be available for regular Daily Special songs. These are even harder than other EXPERT mode songs!


From Wednesday, May 13 to Tuesday, May 19, we will be giving away a Love Gems a day as login bonuses for a total of 7 days.

As we enter our second year of service, we hope you continue to enjoy Love Live! School Idol Festival!


May 12, 2015
