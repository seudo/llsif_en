Over 30 Million Players Pack on Sale!

Date 2016-10-14 五 Tags POST
To celebrate surpassing 30 million players worldwide, we are introducing some special packs the Shop!

Availability:
From 12:00 am on Saturday, October 15 until 11:59 pm on Monday, October 24 UTC.

Available Packs:
- 30 Million Players Pack: μ's
・Scout 11 Scouting Ticket x1
・SSR or above μ's Scouting Ticket x1
・1,000,000 G

- 30 Million Players Pack: Aqours
・Scout 11 Scouting Ticket x1
・SSR or above Aqours Scouting Ticket x1
・1,000,000 G

Item details:
- Scout 11 Scouting Ticket
・A ticket that can be used for one scout on "Scout 11" Honor Student Scouting in either μ's or Aqours mode.
・Use of this ticket is prioritized over other tickets and Love Gems.
・You will receive Honor Student Bonus by using this ticket.

- SSR or above μ's Scouting Ticket
・A ticket that can be used for one scout on "SSR or above μ's Scouting."
・Features SSR or above μ's members that were available from Honor Student Scouting since May 2014 through October 2016. See Details on the "SSR or above μ's Scouting" screen for more details on which members appear.
・This scouting becomes available in Home screen and μ's Scouting menu only when you hold this ticket.
・You will NOT receive Honor Student Bonus by using this ticket.

- SSR or above Aqours Scouting Ticket
・A ticket that can be used for one scout on "SSR or above Aqours Scouting."
・Features SSR or above μ's members that were available from Honor Student Scouting since September 28, 2016. See Details on the "SSR or above Aqours Scouting" screen for more details on which members appear.
・This scouting becomes available in Home screen and Aqours Scouting menu only when you hold this ticket.
・You will NOT receive Honor Student Bonus by using this ticket.

Note:
- Each pack can only be purchased once per person with currency payment. Love Gems cannot be used.
- Scout 11 Scouting Ticket, SSR or above μ's Scouting Ticket, and SSR or above Aqours Scouting Ticket, are all directly sent to your inventory.
- The members available from each scouting includes up to the following additions.

・30 Million Players Pack: μ's
Members up to Baseball series (part 2) scheduled to be added on October 20.

・30 Million Players Pack: Aqours
Members up to additions scheduled on October 24.

- These scouting will be available as long as you hold the tickets. Any changes to the available members will be notified separately.

We hope you enjoy the special packs, and thank you for playing School Idol Festival!
