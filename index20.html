IMPORTANT In-App Purchase Price Change

Due to changing circumstances in foreign exchange rates, Apple Inc. has made adjustments to the prices on the App Store for Canada, Israel, Mexico, New Zealand, Russia, Singapore, South Africa, United Arab Emirates, India, Australia, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, Switzerland, Turkey, and Norway.This means that the in-app purchase prices for Love Live! School Idol Festival has changed for iOS users in the above mentioned countries.In order to bring the in-app purchase prices inline across all supported platforms, we will be adjusting the in-app purchase prices for Android users in the affected countries also.To facilitate this change, we plan to perform maintenance on Friday, January 29, from 8:00 am until 9:00 am UTC , and the changes will be effective immediately after the maintenance.We apologize for the inconvenience caused, and thank you for your understanding.January 27, 20162016/01/27
