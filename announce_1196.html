【Edit 2017/02/15】Upcoming Event

Date 2017-02-14 二 Tags POST
A new event, "One Dreamy Night" will run from 9:00 am on Wednesday, February 15 to 8:00 am Friday, February 24 UTC.

Event Details

During the event, special tokens inside rhythm icons will appear in all Live Shows. Tap these icons to collect the tokens and get some Event Points. Use the tokens you collect to play the event song, which will give you much more Event Points compared to other songs. Depending on how much Event Points you collect, you can win various great prizes!

Note:
- The amount of Event Points you can get from the event song depends on your Score and Combo Rank.
- You will not receive Event Points for Live Shows completed after 8:00 am on Friday, February 24 UTC, even if you start before this time.
- We plan to add the event song as a Hit song sometime after the event has finished.
- There is no event story to go with this event.

Event Achievement Rewards
SR Yoshiko Tsushima [Gothic Dress]
R Kyoko Sasahara [Teacher]
R Satoko Miyama [Teacher]

Event Ranking Rewards
SR Ruby Kurosawa [Gothic Dress]
and more!

Score Ranking Rewards
SS Stickers
S Stickers
N Stickers

Note:
- Yoshiko Tsushima [Gothic Dress] and SR Ruby Kurosawa [Gothic Dress] are available only from their respective reward categories. You can receive up to three each.
- Yoshiko Tsushima [Gothic Dress] and Ruby Kurosawa [Gothic Dress] are being released early for this event, and will be added to Aqours Honor Student scouting and SR or above Aqours Scouting during March.
- SR Members released early for events have a lower appearance rate from Scouting than standard SR Members.
- Teachers are ideal for Practicing, but can't be added to a Team or be Idolized.
Rewards depend on:
- The total number of Event Points you accumulate.
- Your ranking based on your total Event Points.
- Your Event Song Ranking.
- Your Score Ranking on every song you've played during the event.

You can check out the top ten players and the team they've used on the Score Ranking page. Studying them might give you some clues on achieving a higher score!

Note:
- For more details on achievement rewards and ranking rewards, please see the in-game event rewards section.
- Ranking rewards will be distributed after the event has finished.

The event tokens required to play the event song:
EASY - 15
NORMAL - 30
HARD - 45
EXPERT/RANDOM EXPERT - 75

Note:
- Only Expert event songs are available on Random.

There was an error with the following information.
- Expert songs and Random Expert songs are not eligible for the Event Song Ranking.

The correct information is as follows.
- Expert songs and Random Expert songs are eligible for the Event Song Ranking.

- The notes are randomized for Random Expert event songs.
- By increasing the amount of LP you spend on a non-event song, you are able to receive more .
- By increasing the amount of you spend on the event song, you are able to receive more Event Points.
- X4 songs are no longer available from this event onwards.
